About

This is my portfolio website. I plan on including some of my finished projects, as well as some of my thoughts on programming.

There have been very few times in my life where coding wasn't my passion. I love working in Javascript, Python, C++, and any other language to make new ideas real.

What I love most is the beauty of simplicity: finding the minimal amount of code to make a statement is my goal.
